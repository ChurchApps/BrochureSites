---

## Design Notes

- **Brand Colors:** Use purple/blue gradient (#667eea to #764ba2) consistently
- **Photography Style:** Bright, natural, authentic, diverse representation
- **Mood:** Confident, modern, simplified, church-friendly
- **Avoid:** Overly staged photos, stock photo "fakeness", cluttered backgrounds

---

## Recommended Image Sizes

- **Hero images:** 1920x1080px minimum
- **Feature images:** 1200x800px
- **Icons/graphics:** SVG format or 500x500px PNG
- **Screenshots:** Native app resolution
- **Logos:** Vector (SVG) preferred
