ChurchApps Ambassador Covenant

Helping churches help each other through open-source tools

Effective Date: _______________________

Welcome to the ChurchApps Ambassador family!

This covenant outlines our mutual commitments as we partner together to help churches worldwide discover and effectively use free, open-source ministry tools. This is not a legal contract, but a ministry agreement built on trust, integrity, and shared mission.

1. Our Shared Mission

ChurchApps exists to lower barriers so churches can focus on people, not software. As an Ambassador, you join a global network committed to:

  • Helping churches discover and adopt free, open-source ministry tools
  • Providing local, peer-to-peer support and learning communities
  • Sharing feedback and needs with the ChurchApps core development team
  • Building a culture of collaboration, generosity, and contribution
  • Never charging churches for help, training, or support

3. Your Role & Authority

As a ChurchApps Ambassador, you ARE authorized to:

  • Represent ChurchApps locally in your designated region
  • Host user group meetings, trainings, and events under the ChurchApps name
  • Use ChurchApps branding, logos, and marketing materials (per brand guidelines)
  • Moderate and lead your local ChurchApps user group community
  • Share feedback, feature requests, and regional insights with the core team
  • Recruit volunteers to help with local events and activities
  • Connect churches with appropriate ChurchApps products and resources

You are NOT authorized to:

  • Make financial commitments or enter contracts on behalf of ChurchApps
  • Charge any fees for ChurchApps-related services, support, or training
  • Modify, fork, or rebrand ChurchApps software for commercial purposes
  • Speak publicly on behalf of ChurchApps without prior approval on sensitive topics
  • Grant official partnerships or endorsements to third parties
  • Access or share confidential ChurchApps internal information

4. Your Commitments

As a ChurchApps Ambassador, I commit to:

4.1 Ministry Activity

Host at least ONE user group activity per quarter (every 3 months). Activities include: user group meetings, training sessions, office hours, hack nights, or seminars.
Submit a brief quarterly report sharing activities, churches helped, challenges faced, and insights gained.
Maintain an active local communication channel (Facebook group, WhatsApp, Discord, email list, etc.) for your user group.

4.2 Integrity & Service

NO CHARGING POLICY - This is absolutely critical:
I will NEVER charge churches, ministries, or individuals for ChurchApps-related help, training, setup, support, or consulting services.
I will NEVER sell paid services that bundle ChurchApps products or represent them as premium offerings.
I understand that all ChurchApps support and training must be provided freely, just as the software itself is free.
I understand that violating this policy will result in immediate removal from the Ambassador program.
Represent ChurchApps with integrity, honesty, and Christian character.
Be honest about my limitations - I'm an organizer and connector, not expected to be a technical expert.
Direct technical questions I can't answer to the appropriate ChurchApps support channels.
Respect the open-source nature of ChurchApps and encourage contribution back to the community.

4.3 Christian Faith & Conduct

I affirm that I am a follower of Jesus Christ and actively involved in Christian ministry.
I commit to representing Christ and ChurchApps with humility, grace, and servant leadership.
I will maintain a welcoming, inclusive environment in my user group that reflects Christian love.
I will not use my ambassador platform for personal theological or political agendas unrelated to ChurchApps' mission.

4.4 Communication & Monitoring

I understand that emails sent from my @churchapps.org address may be monitored to ensure program integrity and prevent abuse.
I will use my ambassador email professionally and only for ChurchApps-related communications.
I will respond to reasonable requests from the ChurchApps core team within 7 business days.
If I need to pause or step down from my role, I will notify the ChurchApps team at least 30 days in advance when possible.

4.5 Data & Privacy

I will respect the privacy of churches and individuals I interact with through this role.
I will not share contact information of churches or users without their permission.
I will comply with applicable data protection laws (GDPR, etc.) in my region.

5. ChurchApps' Commitments to You

ChurchApps commits to providing you with:

5.1 Resources & Support

  • A regional email address (e.g., yourregion@churchapps.org) with forwarding to your personal email
  • Access to the private Ambassador Resource Hub with guides, templates, and brand assets
  • User Group Starter Package and event planning resources
  • Profile on the ChurchApps website with badge recognition system
  • Access to a private Ambassador community (Facebook group) for peer support
  • Monthly check-ins during your first quarter, then quarterly ongoing

5.2 Recognition & Community

  • Public recognition on our website and in communications
  • Achievement badges for milestones (first meeting, churches helped, contributions, etc.)
  • Opportunities to share your story and insights with the broader ChurchApps community
  • Connection to a global network of like-minded church leaders

5.3 Reasonable Expectations

  • This is a volunteer position - we will not ask more of you than is reasonable
  • We respect your time and other ministry commitments
  • We will provide reasonable notice for any program changes
  • We will listen to and consider your feedback on the program

6. Term & Termination

6.1 Initial Term

This covenant begins on the date signed and continues for one (1) year, with automatic renewal unless either party chooses to end the relationship.

6.2 Voluntary Departure

You may step down at any time by providing 30 days written notice when possible. We understand that life circumstances change. This is stewardship, not a lifetime appointment.

6.3 ChurchApps-Initiated Termination

ChurchApps reserves the right to remove an ambassador for:

  • Charging for Services: Any violation of the "no charging" policy (immediate removal)
  • Inactivity: Missing two (2) consecutive quarterly reports without communication
  • Misconduct: Behavior that brings disrepute to ChurchApps or violates Christian conduct standards
  • Breach of Trust: Misusing access, sharing confidential information, or abusing authority
  • Regional Changes: If we restructure regional coverage or close a region

6.4 Upon Termination

When this covenant ends (for any reason):

  • Your @churchapps.org email will be deactivated within 7 days
  • Your profile will be removed from the website
  • Access to private Ambassador resources will be revoked
  • You will be removed from the private Ambassador community
  • You must cease representing yourself as a ChurchApps Ambassador
  • You are welcome to remain part of the general ChurchApps community

7. Quarterly Reporting Requirements

Every 90 days, you will submit a simple report including:

  1. Number of activities/meetings hosted
  2. Number of churches helped or engaged
  3. Top 2-3 recurring needs or challenges you're seeing
  4. One surprising win or insight
  5. One idea worth exploring
  6. Optional: Photos, testimonials, stories to share

Reports are due: March 31, June 30, September 30, December 31 each year

8. Expectations & Guardrails

DO:

  • Encourage collaboration and contribution to open-source
  • Keep the tone welcoming, humble, and focused on helping
  • Share honest feedback about what's working and what's not
  • Celebrate wins and progress, even small ones
  • Ask questions when you're unsure
  • Focus on peer support and learning, not being the expert

DON'T:

  • Sell services inside the user group (or anywhere related to ChurchApps)
  • Claim authority you don't have
  • Pressure churches to adopt specific tools or approaches
  • Make promises about features or timelines you can't control
  • Use the platform for personal business promotion

9. Intellectual Property

All ChurchApps software, trademarks, logos, and branding remain the property of ChurchApps. You may use these materials in accordance with brand guidelines while serving as an ambassador, but all rights revert upon termination.

10. Limitation of Liability

You understand and agree that:

  • You are an independent volunteer, not an employee or contractor of ChurchApps
  • ChurchApps is not responsible for your actions, advice, or recommendations to churches
  • You provide support and guidance at your own discretion and churches use it at their own risk
  • ChurchApps provides software "as is" and you should direct churches to official terms of service

11. Modifications to This Covenant

ChurchApps may update this covenant with 30 days notice. Continued service as an ambassador after notification constitutes acceptance of changes. Material changes will require re-signing.
