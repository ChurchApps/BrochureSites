  • {REGION} - City/region they're serving
  • {REGION_EMAIL} - Their ambassador email prefix (e.g., "korea")
  • {LANGUAGES} - Languages they speak
  • {DATE} - Relevant date
  • {PERSONAL_EMAIL} - Their personal email
  • {CALENDLY_LINK} - Booking link
  • {DOCUSIGN_LINK} - Agreement signing link
  • {TEAM_MEMBER_NAME} - Staff member name
