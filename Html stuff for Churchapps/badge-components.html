Badge Awarding Guidelines

Automatic vs Manual

  • Automatic: Ambassador, First Meeting (with photo proof)
  • Manual Review: All others require quarterly report review and verification
  • Team Awarded: Platinum tier badges require team decision

Verification Process

  1. Ambassador submits quarterly report with evidence
  2. Team reviews claims and supporting materials (photos, links, testimonials)
  3. Badges awarded and profile updated
  4. Email notification sent to ambassador celebrating new badge

Badge Revocation

Badges can be revoked if:

  • Evidence was fabricated or misleading
  • Ambassador violated covenant (especially charging policy)
  • Ambassador status is terminated
